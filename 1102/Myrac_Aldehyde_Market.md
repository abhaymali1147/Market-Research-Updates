This reaction leads to two mixtures: one with para-position and the other with interposition. Myrac Aldehyde is primarily used as a <strong>freshener</strong> and <strong>flavoring agent</strong> in various industries, including the <strong>cosmetic</strong>, <strong>food</strong>, and <strong>fragrance</strong> sectors.</p>
