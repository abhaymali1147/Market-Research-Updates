 <p>N,N-Diethylethylenediamine (CAS 100-36-7) is a colorless and transparent liquid with a molecular formula of C6H16N2. It is used as an intermediate of fine chemicals to produce anti-arrhythmic drug procainamide, local anesthetics dibucaine hydrochloride and enzyme Yinin. It is also used in the production of auxiliary products, for example, in the preparation of BCH.</p>
