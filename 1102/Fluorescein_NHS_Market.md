<h1>Fluorescein-NHS Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>The <strong>Fluorescein-NHS (N-Hydroxysuccinimide)</strong> market focuses on the trade and application of amine-reactive derivatives of fluorescein dye. These derivatives are widely utilized as labeling agents for antibodies, proteins, and probes in various fluorescence-based assays, including fluorescence microscopy, flow cytometry, immunofluorescence, Western blotting, and ELISA. The NHS-ester form of fluorescein is highly efficient in labeling primary amines such as lysine side chains in purified proteins, making it an essential tool in biochemical and clinical research.</p>
