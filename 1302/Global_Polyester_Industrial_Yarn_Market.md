 <p>Report Overview</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Polyester industrial yarn is a fiber made of PET as raw material, through solid-phase polymerization, melt spinning and post-processing. Polyester industrial yarn is an important raw material for technical textiles. Polyester industrial filaments are widely used by various industrial textile manufacturers for their advantages of high strength, high modulus, dimensional stability, abrasion resistance, aging resistance, chemical resistance, fatigue resistance, and good processing performance.</p>
