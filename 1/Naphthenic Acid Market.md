<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Naphthenic acid refers to a group of carboxylic acids produced during the refining of petroleum. These acids are primarily composed of saturated cyclic hydrocarbons and find applications in various industries such as chemicals, oil, and lubricants.</p>
