<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>NBR foam, also known as Nitrile rubber foam, is a type of synthetic rubber produced through the copolymerization of acrylonitrile and butadiene. It is widely used for insulation and sealing applications in various industries due to its excellent resistance to oils, chemicals, and heat.</p>
