<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Natural coir fiber, derived from the husk of coconuts, is a versatile and sustainable material used in various industries such as agriculture, geotextiles, and home decor. With its biodegradable and eco-friendly properties, coir fiber has gained popularity as a preferred alternative to synthetic materials. The rising awareness of environmental sustainability has further propelled the demand for natural coir fiber products.</p>
