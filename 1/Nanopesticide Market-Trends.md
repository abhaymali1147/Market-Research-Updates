<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nanopesticides are nanoparticle-based formulations designed to enhance the efficacy and target specificity of traditional pesticides while minimizing environmental impacts. These nano-sized particles can improve pesticide delivery, stability, and uptake by target pests or pathogens.</p>
