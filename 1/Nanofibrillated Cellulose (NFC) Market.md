<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nanofibrillated Cellulose (NFC) is a sustainable biomaterial derived from cellulose fibers that have been mechanically disintegrated into nanoscale dimensions, offering superior strength and unique properties. This innovative material finds applications in various industries, including packaging, biomaterials, composites, and more.</p>
