<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Natural diatomite, also known as diatomaceous earth, is a naturally occurring sedimentary rock that is composed primarily of the fossilized remains of diatoms, a type of algae. It is valued for its high porosity and abrasive properties, making it a versatile material used in various industrial applications.</p>
