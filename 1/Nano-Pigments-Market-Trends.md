<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nano pigments are extremely small particles that are used in various industries for their unique properties and applications. These nano-sized pigments offer enhanced color strength, improved durability, and better dispersion compared to traditional pigments. The rise in demand for high-performance pigments across sectors like paints and coatings, cosmetics, and textiles has accelerated the growth of the Nano Pigments market.</p>
