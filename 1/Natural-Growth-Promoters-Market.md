<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Natural Growth Promoters entail products or substances that stimulate growth and development in plants, animals, and humans in a natural and sustainable manner. These promoters typically harness natural components such as beneficial microbes, enzymes, and botanical extracts to enhance growth processes.</p>
