<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Complex-Oxide Nanomaterials are nanostructures composed of multiple metal oxides, exhibiting unique magnetic, optical, and catalytic properties due to their high surface area-to-volume ratio. These materials have applications in various industries, including electronics, catalysis, and medicine.</p>
