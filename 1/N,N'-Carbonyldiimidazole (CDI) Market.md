<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>N,N'-Carbonyldiimidazole (CDI) is a versatile compound widely used in organic synthesis for the modification of various compounds. It acts as a coupling reagent in peptide synthesis and is essential in various chemical processes due to its reactivity and stability. The compound plays a crucial role in the pharmaceutical, agricultural, and material science industries. Its unique properties make it a valuable component in numerous applications.</p>
