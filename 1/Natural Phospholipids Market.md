<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Natural Phospholipids are essential lipid molecules that are found in all biological membranes, playing a crucial role in maintaining cell structure and function. These naturally occurring phospholipids are vital for various physiological processes in humans and animals, making them integral components of the nutraceutical and pharmaceutical industries. With a growing emphasis on natural and sustainable ingredients, the demand for natural phospholipids continues to increase in diverse applications.</p>
