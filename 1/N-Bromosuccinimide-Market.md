<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>N-Bromosuccinimide, commonly referred to as NBS, is a white crystalline solid that is widely used as a reagent in organic synthesis to brominate alkenes selectively. It is a versatile compound known for its role in various chemical reactions and is a key component in the pharmaceutical and chemical industries, among others.</p>
