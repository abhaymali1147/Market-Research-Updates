<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nanoimprint materials refer to a class of materials used in the nanoimprint lithography process, an advanced nanotechnology method for creating nanoscale patterns and structures.</p>
<p>The process involves transferring a pattern from a mold to a substrate using mechanical deformation, electromagnetic fields, or thermal processes.</p>
