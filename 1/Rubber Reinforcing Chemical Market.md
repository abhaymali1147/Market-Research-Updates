<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Rubber reinforcing chemicals refer to a range of additives used in the rubber industry to improve the mechanical properties of rubber compounds such as tear strength, tensile strength, abrasion resistance, etc. These chemicals play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and durability of rubber products.</p>
