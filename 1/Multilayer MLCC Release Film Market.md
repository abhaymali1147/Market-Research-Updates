<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Multilayer MLCC Release Film refers to the thin film that is used during the manufacturing process of Multilayer Ceramic Chip Capacitors (MLCCs) for easy release from the substrate. This film plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and precision of MLCC production processes. The development of advanced release films has significantly enhanced the reliability and quality of MLCCs, catering to the rising demand for these components across various industries globally.</p>
