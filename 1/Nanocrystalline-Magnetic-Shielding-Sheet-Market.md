<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nanocrystalline Magnetic Shielding Sheets are advanced materials used to shield against electromagnetic interference (EMI) and reduce the impact of external magnetic fields on sensitive electronic components. These ultra-thin sheets provide superior performance in high-frequency applications due to their unique nanoscale structure and magnetic properties.</p>
