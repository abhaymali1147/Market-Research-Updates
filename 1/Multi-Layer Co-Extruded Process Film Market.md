<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Multi-Layer Co-Extruded Process Films are advanced packaging materials produced by combining multiple layers of different polymers during the extrusion process. These films offer enhanced barrier properties and strength, making them ideal for various applications in industries like food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and more.</p>
