<p>Nanofilms are thin films with nanoscale thickness, typically ranging from 1 to 100 nanometers, used in various industries for their unique properties and applications. These ultra-thin films exhibit exceptional characteristics such as high surface area-to-volume ratio, enhanced mechanical strength, and improved optical properties.</p>
