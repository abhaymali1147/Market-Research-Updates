<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nanofiltration membrane technology is a process that separates and removes various contaminants from water by utilizing nanoscale filtration membranes, allowing for improved purification and treatment efficiency. These membranes are engineered to exhibit specific filtration characteristics, enabling the removal of particles, organic molecules, and other impurities at the nanometer scale.</p>
