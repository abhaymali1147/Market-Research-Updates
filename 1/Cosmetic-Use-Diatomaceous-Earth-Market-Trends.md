<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Diatomaceous Earth, commonly referred to as DE, is a naturally occurring, soft sedimentary rock that is easily crumbled into a fine white powder. It is widely used in the cosmetics industry for its absorbent and abrasive properties that make it an excellent ingredient in various skincare and beauty products.</p>
