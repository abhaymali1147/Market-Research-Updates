<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Natural Cosmetic Preservatives are ingredients used in cosmetic products to extend their shelf life and prevent microbial contamination. These preservatives are derived from natural sources such as plants, herbs, and essential oils, providing a safer alternative to synthetic preservatives while maintaining product efficacy and safety.</p>
