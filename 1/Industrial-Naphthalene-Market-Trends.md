<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Industrial Naphthalene is a white solid substance derived from coal tar or crude oil that is primarily used in the production of phthalic anhydride, mothballs, and other chemical compounds. It serves as a key raw material in various industries due to its unique properties and applications.</p>
