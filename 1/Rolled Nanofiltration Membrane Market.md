<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Rolled Nanofiltration Membranes are advanced filtration technologies that offer precise separation capabilities on a molecular level. These membranes are commonly used in various industries for applications such as water purification, wastewater treatment, and desalination processes. They enable the selective passage of specific molecules while blocking others, providing a highly efficient and cost-effective solution for filtration needs.</p>
