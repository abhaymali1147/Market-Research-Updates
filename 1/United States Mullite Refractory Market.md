<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mullite refractory is a type of material commonly used in high-temperature applications such as furnaces and kilns due to its excellent thermal shock resistance and high-temperature stability. It is characterized by its low thermal expansion coefficient and resistance to chemical corrosion, making it a preferred choice in industries requiring stringent refractory materials.</p>
