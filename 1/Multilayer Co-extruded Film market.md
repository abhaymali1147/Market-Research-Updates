<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Multilayer co-extruded films are advanced packaging materials composed of multiple layers of polymer resins that are extruded together to form a single film. These films offer superior barrier properties, high strength, and versatility, making them ideal for various applications in the packaging industry.</p>
