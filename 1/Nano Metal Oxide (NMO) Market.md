<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nano Metal Oxides (NMOs) are inorganic materials with particle sizes in the nanometer range. These materials have unique properties due to their small size and high surface area to volume ratio, making them desirable for various applications in industries such as electronics, healthcare, energy, and more.</p>
