<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>N-Acetyl-DL-Phenylalanine, also known as NADL, is a synthetic form of phenylalanine that combines both D and L forms. It is commonly used in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries for its potential health benefits.</p>
