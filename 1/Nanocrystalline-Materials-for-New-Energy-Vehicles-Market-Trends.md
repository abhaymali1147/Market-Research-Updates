<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Nanocrystalline materials refer to materials with grains in the nanometer range, offering unique properties due to their small grain size and high surface area to volume ratio. In the context of New Energy Vehicles, these materials play a vital role in enhancing energy efficiency and overall performance.</p>
