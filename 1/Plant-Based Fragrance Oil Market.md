<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Plant-Based Fragrance Oils are derived from natural plant sources such as flowers, herbs, spices, and fruits. These oils are widely used in perfumes, candles, skincare products, and aromatherapy due to their aromatic properties and environmental benefits. The extraction of plant-based fragrance oils involves methods like steam distillation, cold pressing, or solvent extraction, ensuring a sustainable and eco-friendly alternative to synthetic fragrances.</p>
