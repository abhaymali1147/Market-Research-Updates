<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Multilayer laminated films refer to packaging materials constructed by combining multiple layers of different materials to achieve specific properties such as barrier protection, flexibility, and durability. These films find extensive applications in various industries, including food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p>
