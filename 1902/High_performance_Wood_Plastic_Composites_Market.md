 <p>High-performance Wood Plastic Composites (WPC) are composite materials made from wood fibers and thermoplastics. These materials are designed to offer superior performance in terms of durability, strength, and resistance to environmental factors such as moisture, UV radiation, and temperature changes. The wood fibers used in WPC can be sourced from a variety of wood species, including hardwoods and softwoods. The thermoplastics used in WPC can include polyethylene, polypropylene, and PVC.</p>
