 <p>The prefabricated gold tin aluminum nitride ceramic heat sink is a heat sink material formed by prefabricating a micron-sized gold tin film in a specific area after the aluminum nitride ceramic substrate is metallized. The prefabricated gold tin aluminum nitride ceramic heat sink is mainly composed of solder layer, structural layer, anti-oxidation protective layer, thermal conduction layer, conductive layer, diffusion barrier layer, etc. It adopts PVD technology, precision processing technology, etc. to achieve its low thermal resistance and low stress package applications. The prefabricated gold tin aluminum nitride ceramic heat sink has the characteristics of low thermal resistance and low stress, and is mainly used in the manufacture of fiber-optic coupling modules and laser pump sources, and then used in semiconductor lasers, optical communications and other fields.</p>
