 <p><strong>Agricultural Mineral Oil</strong> refers to a refined petroleum-based oil used in agriculture as a pesticide, adjuvant, or crop protection agent. It is primarily utilized to control pests such as mites, scales, and aphids by smothering them and disrupting their life cycle. These oils are also employed as carriers for herbicides and fungicides, improving the effectiveness of the active ingredients. Agricultural mineral oils are valued for being non-toxic to plants when applied correctly and are widely used in both organic and conventional farming practices.</p>
