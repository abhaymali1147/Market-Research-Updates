<h1>Cosmetic Use Diatomaceous Earth Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Cosmetic Use Diatomaceous Earth (DE) is a naturally occurring, silica-rich sedimentary rock composed of fossilized remains of diatoms. It is widely used in cosmetic products for its ultra-absorbent and exfoliating properties. Due to its porous nature, DE is an effective ingredient in deodorants, antiperspirants, and face masks, helping to control moisture and oil levels while providing gentle exfoliation.</p>
