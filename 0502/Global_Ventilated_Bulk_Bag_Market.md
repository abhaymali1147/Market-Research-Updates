 <p>A ventilated bulk bag, also known as a breathable or mesh bulk bag, is a flexible intermediate bulk container (FIBC) designed to transport and store products that require airflow to maintain freshness and quality. These bags are typically made with woven polypropylene and feature strategically placed ventilation strips or mesh panels. Ventilated bulk bags are ideal for agricultural products like potatoes, onions, and firewood, as they allow moisture and heat to dissipate, preventing mold growth and spoilage. Durable and reusable, they support efficient storage and transport, making them popular in farming, food processing, and related industries.</p>
