 <p>Cesium fluoride (CsF) is a chemical compound composed of cesium and fluorine. It is a white, crystalline solid that is highly soluble in water. Cesium fluoride is used in various industrial applications, including as a catalyst in organic chemistry, in the production of specialty glasses, and as a source of fluoride in chemical processes. It is also employed in some nuclear applications due to its ability to form stable compounds with certain metals. Cesium fluoride is highly reactive, especially with water, and should be handled with caution.</p>
