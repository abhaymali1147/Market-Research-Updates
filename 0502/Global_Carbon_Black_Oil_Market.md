CBO is a complex mixture of hydrocarbons, including aromatic compounds, and typically has a high carbon content. It can be used as a fuel or as a feedstock for further refining processes. Additionally, CBO is sometimes employed in the production of other chemicals or as a raw material in industries such as energy generation or petrochemicals.
