 <p><strong>Pavement Sealers</strong> are protective coatings applied to asphalt or concrete surfaces to extend their lifespan and improve their appearance. These sealers form a barrier that shields pavement from damage caused by water, UV rays, oils, chemicals, and general wear and tear. Commonly used for driveways, parking lots, and roadways, pavement sealers help to prevent cracks, potholes, and surface erosion, thereby reducing maintenance costs. They come in different types, such as coal tar-based, asphalt-based, and acrylic-based sealers, each suited to specific applications and offering varying levels of durability and environmental impact.</p>
