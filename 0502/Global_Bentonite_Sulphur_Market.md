 <p><strong>Bentonite Sulphur</strong> is a type of fertilizer that combines elemental sulfur with bentonite clay. Bentonite acts as a binding agent that, when applied to soil, swells and disintegrates into fine particles, enhancing the dispersion of sulfur. This property allows for gradual sulfur release, making it a valuable nutrient for sulfur-deficient soils and improving nutrient availability for plants.</p>
