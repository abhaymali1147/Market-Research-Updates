 <p><strong>Grout Bags</strong> are flexible, cone-shaped bags used to apply grout, mortar, or other viscous materials in precise locations, especially in hard-to-reach or narrow spaces. They work similarly to pastry bags, allowing controlled and accurate dispensing of grout between tiles, stones, bricks, or in masonry joints. Grout bags are typically made of durable, waterproof materials like rubberized fabric or vinyl and may have a metal or plastic tip for directing the grout flow. They are popular in construction and masonry for minimizing mess and improving application accuracy.</p>
