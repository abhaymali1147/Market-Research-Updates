 <p><strong>Non-Heat Shrink Tubing and Sleeves</strong> are protective coverings used to insulate, protect, or organize wires, cables, and other components without requiring the application of heat to fit them tightly around the object. Unlike heat shrink tubing, which contracts when exposed to heat, non-heat shrink tubing maintains its original size and shape after installation.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>These tubings and sleeves are typically made from materials like PVC, neoprene, silicone, or polyester, offering varying degrees of flexibility, durability, and resistance to environmental factors such as moisture, chemicals, and abrasion. They are commonly used in electrical, automotive, and industrial applications to provide insulation, mechanical protection, and organizational management of cables and wires.</p>
