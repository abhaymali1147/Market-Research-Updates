 <p>Polyimide resin refers to a class of heterocyclic polymers containing imine groups in the main chain of macromolecules, and is one of the organic polymer materials with the best comprehensive performance. It has the characteristics of corrosion resistance, fatigue resistance, damage resistance, impact resistance, low density, low noise, long service life and excellent high and low temperature performance.</p>
