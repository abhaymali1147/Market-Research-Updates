 <p>Recycled cardboard refers to cardboard material that has been processed and manufactured using recovered paper fibers from previously used cardboard or paper products. The recycling process involves collecting used cardboard, breaking it down into fibers through a process of pulping, and then reforming it into new cardboard sheets. Recycled cardboard is commonly used for packaging, shipping boxes, and various other applications, reducing the demand for virgin paper and helping to conserve natural resources.</p>
