 <p><strong>Resin-Coated Sand (RCS)</strong> is a high-performance casting material used in foundry applications. It consists of silica sand coated with a layer of synthetic resin, which acts as a binder. RCS is widely used in shell molding and core-making processes, providing enhanced dimensional accuracy, superior surface finish, and high thermal stability. Its resin coating enables controlled curing and improved strength during the casting process, making it ideal for complex and intricate metal castings.</p>
