 <p><strong>Titanium Etching</strong> is a process used to create patterns or designs on the surface of titanium by applying a chemical solution or abrasive method. This technique is commonly used in industries like electronics, aerospace, and medical devices to modify the surface of titanium for purposes such as improving adhesion, creating decorative finishes, or achieving specific material properties. Etching can be done through wet chemical etching, laser etching, or other methods depending on the desired result.</p>
