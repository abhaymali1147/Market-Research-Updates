 <p>A <strong>Tubular Gauge Glass</strong> is a type of sight glass used to visually monitor the level of liquid or fluid within a container, such as a boiler, tank, or vessel. It consists of a long, transparent tube made from materials like glass, acrylic, or borosilicate, which is mounted vertically on the side of the vessel. The liquid level inside the tube corresponds to the level of the liquid in the container, allowing operators to easily see and monitor the fluid levels.</p>
